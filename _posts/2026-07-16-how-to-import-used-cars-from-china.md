## Why Source Used Cars from China

China offers a large inventory of sedans, SUVs, and new energy vehicles (NEVs) at competitive FOB prices. Many African, Middle East, and Southeast Asian dealers source from Guangzhou and South China ports for retail replenishment or fleet orders.

## The 6-Step Export Process

1) Search inventory and shortlist units. 2) Submit RFQ for FOB/CIF quote (typically within 24 hours). 3) Negotiate dealer rates. 4) Pay 30% deposit to lock the unit. 5) FCL/LCL shipment with full export docs. 6) Pick up at destination port. Always request a 120-point inspection report and photo/video before deposit.
