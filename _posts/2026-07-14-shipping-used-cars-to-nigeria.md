## CIF to Lagos (Apapa)

CIF quotes include sea freight to Apapa and basic insurance. Typical timeline from deposit to port arrival is 30–45 days depending on vessel schedule and inspection lead time. Confirm SONCAP-aligned specs before loading to avoid clearance delays.

## Mixed SUV Lots for Retail

Many Lagos dealers order 3–5 unit mixed lots (Toyota RAV4, Honda CR-V, BYD SUVs) to replenish retail inventory. Request itemized FOB per unit plus consolidated CIF for the container.
