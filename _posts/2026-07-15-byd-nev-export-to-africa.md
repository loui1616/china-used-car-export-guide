## Popular BYD Models for Export

BYD Seal, Song L EV, Qin Plus, and Sealion 07 DM-i are among the most requested NEV models for West and East African markets. Confirm battery health, charging standard, and local compliance before ordering.

## Shipping & Documentation

Most NEV exports use FCL (20GP/40HQ) from South China ports. Exporters should provide commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, and pre-shipment inspection where required.
